Juan Manuel Enrique

CEO & Co-Founder

Juan Manuel Enrique is an experienced computer engineer and data scientist, with a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Purdue University. As an active professor at the Costa Rica Institute of Technology, he has specialized in data science and has worked as a data mining and AI engineer at Google . With his deep expertise and knowledge, Juan brings valuable insights and leadership to Datalitica's services.

Daniela Rivera

CTO & Co-Founder

With over 18 years of experience in software development team administration, Daniela holds a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering and a Master's degree in Business Administration from the Costa Rica Institute of Technology (TEC). She also has a Master's degree in Information Technology from Purdue University, where she worked during her studies. Daniela has worked closely with education systems throughout her career, bringing valuable insights to her work in technology consulting.
